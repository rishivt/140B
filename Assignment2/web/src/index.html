Chamelodors

The Product

Chamelodors is a new company with a product that is seeking to target the cigar and cannabis market with a premium smart and stylish humidor for both storage and display. This humidor will be fully automated - no more fussing with humidification packs. The humidity settings can be changed either through the control panel or on our mobile app. The interface includes easy-to-read gauges and intuitive controls such that anyone can simply store and display their cigars or cannabis with confidence and ease of mind.


We are made for the smoking enthusiast. We hope you have the best experience smoking your favorite product EVERY time. You can interface with a Chamelodor in person or our app to change the humidity and temperature settings exactly the way you want them- whenever you want them.


Maximize the life of your favorite products with a Chamelodor.
The Key Value Proposition

We are engineering a high-quality, portable smart humidor for your cigar and cannabis needs. Simply place your products within our case when they need to be stored, set your preferred humidity and temperature and rest easy.
